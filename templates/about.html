A BRIEF HISTORY
As of January 2025, I am an alumni of the ZipCode Wilmington Data Engineering cohort. ZipCode is a very selective bootcamp. I was one of 10 out of over 100 applicants to pass the assessment and interview process.
The cohort was 13 weeks long, with an 80-100 hour weekly course load. During this time, I also faced early on-set cataracts and kept up despite worsening vision and two surgeries.

Before ZipCode, I have been a long haul truck driver, a medical biller, patient coordinator, medical receptionist, and medical assistant. I enjoy training, tutoring, and developing systems and processes.
